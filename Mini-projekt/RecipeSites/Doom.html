Doom Eternal Demon-Slaying Elixir (non edible)

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup dried elderflowers
  • 1/4 cup dried yarrow
  • 1/4 cup dried lavender
  • 1/4 cup dried lemon balm
  • 2 tablespoons witch hazel
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 5 drops tea tree oil
  • 5 drops peppermint oil

Doom Eternal Hell Knight Serum

  1. In a small saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the elderflowers, yarrow, lavender, and lemon balm to the water. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a heat-safe bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. Add the witch hazel, honey, tea tree oil, and peppermint oil to the bowl and stir to combine.
  5. Transfer the elixir to a clean glass bottle or jar with a tight-fitting lid.
  6. To use, apply a small amount of the elixir to a cotton pad and swipe it over your face and neck.
  7. Use the elixir daily to purify and protect your skin from demonic forces.

Note: Essential oils are potent and should be used with caution. If you have sensitive skin, you may want to dilute the essential oils further by adding more carrier oils or water to the mixture. It is also recommended to perform a patch test before using the elixir on your face to ensure that you do not have any adverse reactions.

Doom Eternal Hell Knight Serum (up to you if its edible)

  • 1/2 cup aloe vera gel
  • 1/4 cup rose water
  • 1/4 cup witch hazel
  • 1 tablespoon argan oil
  • 1 tablespoon glycerin
  • 10 drops frankincense essential oil
  • 10 drops myrrh essential oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the aloe vera gel, rose water, witch hazel, argan oil, and glycerin until well combined.
  2. Add the frankincense essential oil and myrrh essential oil to the bowl and stir to incorporate.
  3. Transfer the serum to a clean glass bottle or jar with a tight-fitting lid.
  4. To use, apply a small amount of the serum to your face and neck after cleansing and toning.
  5. Use the serum daily to nourish and protect your skin from the formidable Hell Knights of Doom Eternal.

Note: Essential oils are potent and should be used with caution. If you have sensitive skin, you may want to dilute the essential oils further by adding more carrier oils or water to the mixture. It is also recommended to perform a patch test before using the serum on your face to ensure that you do not have any adverse reactions.

Doom Eternal Demon Skewer

  • 1 pound beef tenderloin or sirloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 zucchini, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Thread the beef, bell peppers, onion, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes onto skewers, alternating between the different ingredients.
  4. Brush the skewers with the balsamic vinegar mixture.
  5. Grill the skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the beef is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Serve the skewers hot off the grill. Enjoy!
