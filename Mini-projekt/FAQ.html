FAQ

  • Q: What's the best way to cook a rubber chicken?

    • A: We recommend simmering it in a pot of boiling water for several hours, or until it reaches an internal temperature of at least 165 degrees F. It may not be the most delicious, but it will definitely be done!

  • Q: Can I use your recipes to impress my significant other?

    • A: Yes, but we cannot guarantee the results. We suggest using the recipe as a starting point and adding your own personal touch to make it truly unique.

  • Q: Can I substitute tofu for meat in your recipes?

    • A: Sure, but keep in mind that tofu doesn't taste like meat. It has its own flavor and texture. But, who doesn't love a good Tofu Stir Fry?

  • Q: Do you have any recipes for leftovers?

    • A: Yes, we have a whole section dedicated to recipes that can be made with leftovers. Just add some creativity and a sprinkle of magic, and you'll have a whole new dish!
