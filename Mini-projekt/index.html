DOOM

DOOM is a first-person shooter video game developed by id Software and published by Bethesda Softworks. Released on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One on May 13, 2016, it is a reboot of the Doom series and is the first major installment in the series since the release of Doom 3 in 2004. The game is set on Mars in the year 2145, where a Union Aerospace Corporation research facility has been overrun by demons. The player takes control of a space marine, who is tasked with defeating the demons and rescuing the facility's scientists.

DOOM was developed using id Software's id Tech 6 engine, which was built from the ground up for the game. The game's multiplayer component was developed by id Software and Certain Affinity, and features a variety of game modes, including deathmatch, team deathmatch, and capture the flag. The game received positive reviews from critics, who praised its gameplay, graphics, and multiplayer component. It was also a commercial success, selling over 1.5 million copies in its first week of release.

DOOM was followed by a sequel, Doom Eternal, which was released on March 20, 2020. A virtual reality version of the game, titled Doom VFR, was released on October 11, 2017. A Nintendo Switch version of the game was released on November 10, 2017. A mobile version of the game, titled Doom Classic, was released on December 10, 2018. A Nintendo Switch port of Doom Eternal was released on November 22, 2020.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a 2015 action role-playing game developed and published by CD Projekt. Based on The Witcher series of fantasy novels by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski, it is the sequel to the 2011 game The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings and the third main installment in The Witcher video game series, played in an open world with a third-person perspective. The game was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One in May 2015, and for Nintendo Switch in October 2018. It was released for Stadia in December 2019.

The game takes place in a medieval fantasy world based on Slavic mythology. Players control protagonist Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a witcher. Geralt is looking for his adopted daughter, who is on the run from the Wild Hunt, a ghostly procession of eldritch beings. The game's story is non-linear, and players can complete quests and explore the game world in any order. The game features a branching storyline, with players able to make choices that can significantly affect the game's story and ending.

The Witcher 3 received critical acclaim, with praise directed at its story, characters, world design, and combat. It won over 250 game of the year awards, and was named the best game of the decade by several publications. It has sold over 28 million copies worldwide, making it one of the best-selling video games of all time. The game's expansions, Hearts of Stone and Blood and Wine, were also critically acclaimed. A sequel, Cyberpunk 2077, was released in 2020.
